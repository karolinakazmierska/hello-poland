Chido-Fajny

by David Huerta

One of the most famous Expats blogs about Poland. Articles about culture, food, language, history and many, many more. Discover Poland through the eyes of a Mexican!

The Twisted Red Ladybug

by Anda Alexandra

Romanian expat blogger living in Krakow since 2011. Writing about being an expat, a mother outside her own culture but mostly about Poland - what to do, what to see and where to eat in Krakow and other cities. Feel free to join in #thetwistedredladybug madness.

Polish Housewife

by Lois Britton

Traditional Polish recipes, served up with tidbits of folklore, customs, and traditions. Written by Lois Britton, an avid home cook and American expat who spent five years in Poznan.

Indian Girl in Poland

by Doreen Pinto

An Indian Expat Blogger living in Krakow, writing about her life, interests, travel and experience staying in Poland. Loves living in Krakow and learning more and more about this city everyday. A guest blogger at TheKrakowist.com, an online magazine about Krakow.
